INSTRUCTIONS

Good morning!

Your task is to work your way through as much of the material presented as you can in 30 Minutes.

As click on each of the items (the files, letters, PC, phone, etc.) you will find in your office, further information will be provided and/or various situations will be revealed.

For each situation presented you will be asked to indicate how you would respond. Only RED items can be clicked. Her in the lobby, the door will turn a solid red and allow to you click on it and start the game once you have clicked on all the other red items in the room, including the poster on the wall..

After each response, you will be provided with the feedback specific to your response. Be sure to read this feedback carefully as it may help you answer subsequent questions. Points will also be granted for responses that honor our HSBC brand promise and deducted for responses that do not. There are a total of 180 points available.

You don’t need to respond to the tasks in any particular order, nor do you have to complete all tasks specific to any given item before moving on to another. All responses are, however, final. You can return to review already answered questions, but you can't change your responses.

INTRO

Welcome to the Air Canada Global Sales University.

Training is without question – a key metric of success in our globalized world. At Air Canada, we have invested heavily in training in everything from customer soft skills to sophisticated training equipment to teach pilots to fly our newest technology aircraft.

The Global Sales Team at Air Canada is also focused on developing training opportunities that allow our travel partners to build the skills and knowledge needed to be successful.

In a competitive environment such as ours, training becomes even more important as a tool to help our partners better sell us. Customers have a lot of questions and we know counselors do too and we want to make sure you and your team can answer them confidently. That is why the Global Sales team is committed to providing our partners with the tools that build on the extensive skill set and knowledge that counselors have already acquired.

These case studies, and the content shared within the learning modules of the Air Canada Global Sales University demonstrate our commitment to training and skills development. Helping our partners become aware of our extensive product offering allow us to jointly serve our customers, offering them value and choice.

We applaud and appreciate your personal investment in this training program.
